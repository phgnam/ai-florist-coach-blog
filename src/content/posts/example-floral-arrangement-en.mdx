A simple floral arrangement is one of the easiest ways to bring flowers into your home.

## Start with three ingredients

Choose a focal flower, a filler flower, and a bit of greenery.

## Keep the shape loose

Rotate the vase as you place stems so the arrangement feels natural from every angle.

## Finish with water

Trim the stems and place the bouquet in fresh water right away.

That’s it — a small arrangement can still feel elegant and complete.
